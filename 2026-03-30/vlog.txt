=== CÁCH DÙNG TỰ NHIÊN ===

- Honestly, today was rough.
- It was kind of weird.
- Looking back, I'm used to it — nothing special.
- It turns out it was totally worth it!
- I can't wrap my head around this.
- Like, everything was delicious. (like = đầu vế, thay kind of)

=== CẤU TRÚC HAY DÙNG TRONG VLOG ===

1. I didn't expect + sth + to be + adj
   → I didn't expect the food to be this good.

2. It turns out + S + V
   → It turns out the place was worth it.

3. Looking back, ...
   → Looking back, I should have tried it sooner.

4. Honestly, ... — today was rough / it was amazing / I was wrong
